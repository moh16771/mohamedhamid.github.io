Planet Earth: Our Blue Marble

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. While huge volumes of water can be found throughout the Solar System, only Earth sustains liquid surface water.

Atmosphere

Our atmosphere is a mix of 78% nitrogen and 21% oxygen. It protects us from incoming meteoroids and harmful solar radiation.

The Oceans

Water covers about 71% of Earth's surface. These vast oceans are responsible for regulating the planet's climate and supporting millions of species.

Earth rotates on its axis every 23.9 hours and completes an orbit around the Sun in 365.25 days. Its tilt creates our seasons and its magnetic field protects the atmosphere from being stripped away by solar winds.
